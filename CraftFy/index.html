Questions

Things people ask us

Does Craftly actually sound like my brand?

Yes. You fill out a brand profile — products, target audience, tone, do's and don'ts. The AI uses this context every time it generates. It doesn't write generic content; it writes your content.

What types of content does it create?

Social captions (Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook), product descriptions, email newsletters, ad copy, and taglines. More formats are on the roadmap.

Can I edit what the AI writes?

Completely. Every piece is editable inline. If you don't like something, click Regenerate on just that item — nothing else changes. When you're happy, save or export.

What happens next month?

Log back in, your brand profile is saved. Refresh your content — posts, emails, descriptions — in one click. No re-entering anything.

When does Craftly launch?

We're aiming for a Q3 2026 launch. Waitlist members get early access and a 30% discount on their first 3 months.

Is there a free trial?

Waitlist members will receive a 14-day free trial at launch — no credit card needed. After that, you choose a plan.
